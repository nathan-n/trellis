# Trellis — Future Feature Ideas

## Notification System
- Email or push notifications for task assignments, upcoming visits, medication refill dates
- Without notifications, users must remember to check Trellis proactively

## Print/Share Emergency Profile
- Generate a PDF or printable version of the emergency page
- Keep in wallet, on the fridge, or in the car

## Photo Journal / Memory Book
- Capture moments, outings, milestones
- Family keepsake and evidence of engagement for care quality

## Care Plan Templates
- Pre-built task lists for common scenarios (new diagnosis checklist, transitioning to assisted living, hospice preparation)
- Saves new families from starting from zero

## Stage-Aware Guidance
- Tag the patient's current Alzheimer's stage
- Surface relevant resources, what to expect next, and suggested tasks per stage

## Reporting / Analytics
- Trends over time: mood patterns, sleep quality, behavioral changes
- Useful for doctors and for noticing gradual decline

## In-App Messaging
- Simple message thread per circle
- Keeps caregiver coordination out of separate group chats
